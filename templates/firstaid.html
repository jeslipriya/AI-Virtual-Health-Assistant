First Aid Guide 🩹

🚑 First Aid Techniques

  • 🩸 Bleeding: Apply pressure with a clean cloth, elevate the wound, and seek medical help if bleeding doesn’t stop.
  • 🔥 Burns: Cool the burn under running water for 10 minutes, apply a burn gel, and avoid popping blisters.
  • 🤕 Fractures: Immobilize the injured area, avoid moving the limb, and seek medical attention immediately.
  • 🤢 Choking: Perform the Heimlich maneuver (abdominal thrusts) to dislodge the object.
  • 🛑 CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation): If someone is unresponsive and not breathing, start chest compressions (100-120 per minute) and rescue breaths.
  • 🦟 Insect Bites & Stings: Remove stinger if present, apply an ice pack, and use an antihistamine if needed.
  • 🧊 Sprains & Strains: Follow R.I.C.E. (Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation) to reduce swelling and pain.
  • 🚫 Poisoning: Do not induce vomiting unless instructed by medical professionals. Call poison control immediately.
  • 🤕 Head Injuries: Keep the person awake, monitor symptoms, and seek emergency care if they show signs of concussion.
  • 🩺 Heart Attack: Call emergency services, keep the person calm, and give aspirin if advised.

🛍️ First Aid Essentials

  • ✔️ Adhesive bandages (Band-Aids)
  • ✔️ Sterile gauze pads & medical tape
  • ✔️ Antiseptic wipes & hydrogen peroxide
  • ✔️ Antibiotic ointment (Neosporin)
  • ✔️ Pain relievers (Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Paracetamol)
  • ✔️ Tweezers & scissors
  • ✔️ Cold packs (Instant ice packs)
  • ✔️ Thermometer
  • ✔️ Latex-free gloves
  • ✔️ CPR face mask
